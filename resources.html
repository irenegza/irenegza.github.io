Popular Resources

  • Khan Academy

    An online platform offering free courses on a wide range of subjects. Great for supplemental learning in multiple languages.

  • Duolingo

    A popular app for language learning, offering bite-sized lessons in various languages. Ideal for students at all levels.

  • Nearpod

    An interactive lesson platform that allows teachers to engage students with multimedia presentations, quizzes, and collaborative tools.

  • Quizlet

    A versatile tool for creating flashcards and quizzes, useful for vocabulary building and exam prep across languages.

  • Flipgrid

    A video discussion platform where students can share responses and ideas. Encourages verbal practice in bilingual education.

Teacher Tools

  • Edmodo

    A platform for creating a virtual classroom where teachers can share resources, assign work, and connect with students.

  • Google for Education

    Google's suite of tools (Docs, Slides, Classroom) designed for educators to manage classes and collaborate with students.

  • ClassDojo

    An app that fosters communication between teachers, students, and parents, with rewards for positive behavior.
